English

Teamleader is a CRM software that helps you manage your relationships with potential and existing customers. With Teamleader, you can organise your contacts, track your sales opportunities, manage your projects and invoice your services in a single online tool. Teamleader is designed to meet the needs of small and medium-sized businesses that want to grow, optimise their organisation and offer a quality service to their customers.
